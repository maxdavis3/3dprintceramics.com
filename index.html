CAPABILITIES

Ceramic Production Services

01

Zirconia Precision Parts

Yttria-stabilized zirconia (3Y-TZP) for dental bridges, hip ball heads, and oxygen sensors. HIP-densified to 99.9% theoretical density. Superior fracture toughness versus alumina for structural applications.

02

Alumina Insulators

High-purity alumina (≥99.5% Al₂O₃) for electrical insulators, plasma torch components, and wear-resistant guides. Multi-channel internal geometries achievable via binder jetting without core tooling.

03

Silicon Carbide Components

Reaction-bonded and sintered SiC for extreme environments: chemical processing, semiconductor equipment, and ballistic protection. Hardness 2800 HV — twice that of hardened steel.
